Procedure to Cancel Pending Orders During System Failure

If IBTS/KITS or related trading systems are down — whether on the investor side, broker side, or due to a third-party vendor — investors can still manage their orders through the broker’s KATS desk using the steps below.

What to do if the IBTS/KITS is down

In case of IBTS system failure due to any reason — investor’s side, broker’s side, or third-party software vendor’s part — you can call the KATS operator of the broker to:

  1. Get an update regarding your lined-up orders.
  2. Give instructions to the KATS operator to cancel pending orders.
  3. Ask the operator to place new / further orders on your behalf.
  4. Request amendments to any lined-up orders, if required.
✅ Tip: Before you call, keep your Client ID, last 6 digits of CNIC, and symbol / quantity of the order with you so that the KATS operator can verify and execute your instructions quickly.
