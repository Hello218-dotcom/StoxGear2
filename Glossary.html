Investment Glossary

Ask Price
Lowest price a seller accepts.
Bond
Loan to a company or government.
Capital Gain
Profit when selling higher than buying.
Dividend
Share of company profit paid to investors.
IPO
Company sells shares to public first time.
Asset
Anything valuable you own.
Bear Market
Market with falling prices.
Bull Market
Market with rising prices.
Broker
Company that executes trades.
Buy Order
Instruction to purchase shares.
Sell Order
Instruction to sell shares.
Market Order
Buy or sell immediately.
Limit Order
Buy/sell at a set price.
Stop Loss
Sell to prevent large losses.
Portfolio
Collection of investments.
Diversification
Investing in many assets.
Equity
Ownership in a company.
Debt
Borrowed money to be repaid.
ETF
Tradable basket of assets.
Index
Group of stocks tracked together.
Liquidity
How quickly something can sell.
Volatility
Speed of price changes.
Leverage
Using borrowed money to invest.
Yield
Return earned on investment.
Margin
Borrowed funds for trading.
Risk
Chance of losing money.
Return
Money made from investment.
Short Selling
Profit from falling prices.
Blue Chip
Large stable company stock.
Volume
Number of shares traded.
NAV
Value per unit of fund.
