Investment Glossary

Ask Price
Lowest price a seller agrees to accept for a share.
Arbitrage
Profit from price difference of same asset in two markets.
Asset Allocation
Spreading money across investments to reduce risk.
Bear Market
Period when stock prices fall for a long time.
Bond
A loan made to a company or government with interest.
Capital Gain
Profit earned from selling an investment for more.
Compound Interest
Earning interest on both the original and past interest.
Dividend
Company’s profit paid to shareholders.
ETF
Exchange-Traded Fund that tracks an index or sector.
Inflation
Increase in general price levels over time.
IPO
Initial Public Offering—when a company sells shares for the first time.
