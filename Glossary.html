Investment Glossary

Ask Price
Lowest price a seller agrees to accept.
Bond
Loan to a company or government with interest.
Capital Gain
Profit made by selling something at a higher price.
Dividend
Portion of company profit paid to shareholders.
IPO
When a company sells shares to the public for the first time.
Asset
Anything valuable owned.
Bear Market
Market where prices fall.
Bull Market
Market where prices rise.
Broker
Company that trades shares for you.
Buy Order
Instruction to purchase shares.
Sell Order
Instruction to sell shares.
Market Order
Buy or sell at current price.
Limit Order
Buy/sell at a chosen price.
Stop Loss
Auto-sell to prevent loss.
Volatility
Price movement speed.
Liquidity
How fast you can sell.
Portfolio
Your collection of investments.
Equity
Shares of a company.
Debt
Borrowed money.
ETF
Basket of stocks traded like one share.
Index
Group of stocks measured together.
Margin
Borrowed money to trade.
Risk
Chance of losing money.
Return
Money gained from investment.
Blue Chip
Large, stable company.
Yield
Income earned on investment.
Short Selling
Betting that price will fall.
Exchange
Market where stocks trade.
Bid Price
Highest price a buyer offers.
Book Value
Company value on paper.
EPS
Earnings per share.
PE Ratio
Price divided by earnings.
Spread
Difference between buy and sell price.
Volume
Number of shares traded.
Voluntary Corporate Action
Action shareholders may choose.
Mandatory Corporate Action
Action applied to all shareholders.
Bonus Shares
Free shares issued to shareholders.
Rights Issue
Offer to buy more shares at discount.
NAV
Value per share of a fund.
Mutual Fund
Pool of money invested together.
Derivative
Contract based on asset price.
Hedge
Protection against risk.
Inflation
Increase in prices over time.
Deflation
Decrease in prices over time.
