Market Plumbing

What is CDC (Central Depository Company)?

Electronic custody and settlement that reduces risk, paperwork, and delays.

Before CDS

Capital markets prior to CDS

  • Paper certificates: damaged/lost/forged risks.
  • Transfer delays (weeks), stamp duty costs.
  • Heavy manual processing & long dispatch times.
After CDS

Benefits after CDS was incorporated

  • Instant ownership transfers; no stamp duty.
  • Credit of bonus/rights/new issues faster.
  • Lower paperwork & capital/time required.

Investing Styles & Finding Your Fit

Conservative

Capital protection first

Prefer lower risk and stability; willing to trade some growth for peace of mind.

Moderate

Balanced growth

Blend of growth & income; diversification to smooth volatility.

Aggressive

Higher risk, higher return

Pursue strong growth, accept larger swings and possible losses.
